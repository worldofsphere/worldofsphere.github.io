    About

    SPHERE

    Adornment Concepts at Sphere emerge from the heart and end up creating a style statement! Thats the reason why our collections are so loved and treasured by our customers year after year.

    Sonali Shah Sheth, our Creative Director has been at the center of the World of Sphere. Her award winning collections have succeeded in giving a distinct design identity to Sphere as a promising fine jewellery brand from India.

    Allure Collection

    Allure - “Conquer the day with glam”


    An enchanting collection of daily wear jewellery that adds a perfect potion of glamour to the mundane chores of everyday life!

    Meticulously handcrafted by our artisans, and studded with beautiful pear shape diamonds, this collection aims to charm the wearer and prepare her to conquer the day, head on!

    Miraas Collection

    Miraas, a contemporary collection by Sonali Shah Sheth, beautifully encapsulates her experiences through the streets of Turkey.
    Crafted in 14 and 18kt gold using specially formulated enamels, this keepsake collection is an embodiment of the unique “East meets West” – culture of Turkey,
    It is a befitting metaphor to spirit of the woman of today who is both rooted in traditions, yet modern in outlook.
    Chosen as the best Project at the prestigious IIM Ahmedabad Luxury Program, this collection stretches the boundaries of fine gold jewellery design and brings it into the contemporary Wearable Art Space.

    Vinayaka Collection

    Titled “Vinayaka” , the collection comprises of different artisitic forms of Lord Ganesha, the most popular God in Indian Mythology whose blessings are seeked before beginning an important task.

    Designed by Sonali in traditional as well as modern styles, this is a unisex collection that can be worn by men-women, young and old and can serve as an ideal gift option during festivals and special family occasions.

    Eminente Collection

    Presenting Our First Men's Collection: Eminentē

    Inspired by German engineering and finish and Crafted in 18kt gold with diamonds in combination with enamels - this collection aims to give men, a classy and sophisticated style statement.
    Eminentē is specially designed for well travelled and well read men, who may be extremely busy chasing their goals or building the life of their dreams, but who would still make time for small pleasures of life.
